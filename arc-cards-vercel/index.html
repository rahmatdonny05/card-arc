Arc Cards is a community initiative by Domith to celebrate builders in the Arc ecosystem. Each card is a soulbound NFT — permanently linked to your wallet, featuring a unique robot anime character generated from your username.

Click Connect Wallet in the top right. Works with MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, and any EVM-compatible wallet. Arc Testnet (Chain ID 5042002) is added to your wallet automatically.

The community wave is open to Arc ecosystem participants — developers, early testnet users, and active community members on X / Twitter. Enter your username to check eligibility instantly. No account linking required.

Each Arc Card features a unique robot anime character — mech armor, glowing eyes, custom weapons — all deterministically generated from your X username. 8 types, 4 rarities (Common → Legendary). Type your username above to preview yours instantly.
